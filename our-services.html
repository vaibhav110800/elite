Our Services

MANPOWER SERVICES

At Elite Enterprises we have wealth of experience in the area of permanent recruitment. Over a period of more than 2 decades, we have developed high degree of expertise in fulfilling permanent positions. Our team of experienced recruiters ensure that we understand your requirement in detail and conduct innovative head-hunting techniques to deliver right fit candidates on permanent rolls of client organization.



Strategy

Working together with our clients, we evaluate the situation and determine a search strategy for finding the best candidate. An important aspect to this process is a clear understanding of the client’s culture, the position, responsibilities and necessary qualifications needed to do justice to the position. Once the profile is developed, we define our search strategy, including target sectors, companies as well as levels which result in finding the best person for the job.


Candidate Identification and Assessment

We conduct preliminary research for developing an initial candidate list. We contact, assess and validate a short list of potential candidates through telephonic and personal interviews. We then prepare a candidate assessment report, which is then presented to the client for review. When the client is ready to proceed on the list, our team assists with coordinating the interviews as well as taking & incorporating regular feedback.


Search Completion, follow up and hand holding

We consult with our clients to review top candidates and support the selection process. If needed we provide support in the negotiations between the client and candidate. Even after the position is filled, we conduct ongoing follow-up with both client and candidate to ensure success of the hired executive.
